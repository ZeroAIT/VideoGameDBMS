WHAT DRIVES US

We students may be motivated by our own interest in a topic, a prior success in a specific subject, or a desire to please parents or teachers or simply by our own drive to succeed. However, motivation works best when we also have a healthy self-image, are confident in our own abilities and know how to take a step-by-step approach to problem solving.

WHY GAMING?

Gaming is really a workout for your mind disguised as fun.
Studies have shown that playing video games regularly may increase gray matter in the brain and boost brain connectivity. (Gray matter is associated with muscle control, memories, perception, and spatial navigation.)

BENEFITS OF PLAYING VIDEO GAMES

Boosts motivation

Breathes life into education

Gives opportunities to practice.

Speed up response times

Stimulate creativity, focus and visual memory

Improve strategy,leadership and encourages teamwork
